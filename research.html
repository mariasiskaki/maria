Research Interests

I study the analytic and ergodic properties of Gauss and Farey maps associated with Euclidean algorithms, such as
the Even, the Odd, the Backwards, and the Nearest Integer Gauss shifts. Such properties include the distribution
of the periodic points of these maps, mixing rates, Perron-Frobenius operators and analogs of the Gauss problem.
